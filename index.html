About Us

We're more than a club

WiC Oswego is a student-run organization dedicated to supporting, empowering, and connecting women in computing at SUNY Oswego.

Mentorship & Growth

Connect with upperclassmen and industry professionals who can guide your journey in computing.

Community & Belonging

A safe, welcoming space for all women and gender-diverse students in computing fields.

Career & Opportunities

Resume workshops, mock interviews, networking events, and connections to internships and jobs.

Technical Skills

Hackathons, workshops, and collaborative projects to build your skills and portfolio.

Built with heart at Oswego

We believe diversity in tech isn't just important — it's essential. Whether you're a first-year student or a senior researcher, every voice belongs here.
